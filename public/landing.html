Built to be believed

Confidence, quantified.

Anyone can say they're local, green, and fair. The CEIS™ Score is the first number engineered so you don't have to take their word for it — a proprietary algorithm that weighs 47 verified signals through a five-stage pipeline, with four safeguards that make the number mean something.

⚖️

Category-fair weighting

A coffee shop and a law firm create impact in different ways. The algorithm re-weights the Locality, Sustainability, and Transparency pillars to fit each business type — so every score is an honest, apples-to-apples comparison.

🛡️

The Evidence Multiplier

The anti-greenwashing core. Third-party-certified proof amplifies a score; an unverified "trust me" is heavily discounted. A business can't talk its way to a high number — it has to prove it with documentation.

⏳

Time Decay (DAF)

A score reflects right now, not a trophy from years ago. The Decay & Amplification Function holds a score at full strength when fresh, then fades it over a year if the business doesn't renew its verification.

🔐

Identity Confidence (ICS)

Before a score is ever published, the algorithm confirms the business is real — checking signals like its EIN, Secretary-of-State good standing, and Dun & Bradstreet records. No verified identity, no high score.

The exact formula is proprietary and patent-pending to DollarVote PBC. Supporting statistics are modeled estimates.
